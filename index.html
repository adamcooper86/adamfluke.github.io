BipHub is comprehensive solution

BipHub is designed to make gathering, storing, interpreting, and reporting Behavoir Intervention efficient and accurate. It reduces the frustration general education teachers feel about keeping track of another peice of paper, or not being able to give meaningful feedback. It increases the accuracy of the data collected because data can't be lost, or incomplete. Educators are empowered to make effective plan decisions through a user friendly graphing and report suite.
